Cultivation Tips For Onion
Details
Farming Methods
Land
The soil should be well drained, light to medium. Limestone content should not be more than 8%, also electrical conductivity 2 de.C. should not be less than per meter. The ratio should be between 6.1 and 7.5.
the weather
A temperature of 5 to 25 degrees Celsius and a humidity of 70% are favorable for the best growth of onion. High rainfall, high humidity and cloudy weather causes a large incidence of Karpa disease on the onion crop..
Planting time
Kharif: July-August,
Rangada: September-October,
Summer: November- December
Improved varieties
Kharif: Phule Samarth, Baswant 780, Agrifound Dark
Rangda: Baswant 780, Phule Samarth, N-2-4-1
Summer: N-2-4-1, Agrifound Light Red, Arka Niketan
Seeds
3 to 4 kg per acre
nursery
For one acre area, plantation should be done on 0.05 acre area. 10 to 15 cm High, 1 m. Prepare the gadivafe of Rudd and Pureshya Lambi and spread Thiram 2 gm/kg treated seeds in the rows. Kharip seedlings are ready for cultivation in 35 to 40 days and Rangda and Unali Hangama seedlings are ready for cultivation in 45 to 50 days.
Cultivation difference
Kharip, Rangda, Unhaali:15x10 cm.
Balanced Fertilizer
Apply 8 to 10 tons of rotted organic manure 15 days before planting.
40: 20: 20: 20 Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Palash, Sulfur / acre, half of Nitrogen, full Phosphorus, Palash and Sulfur at the time of planting and the remaining Nitrogen in 2 equal installments after 30 and 45 days.
Weed control and intercropping
Oxyfluorphene 1.5 ml on steam three days before planting. Three ml per liter or 3 ml of pendimethylene per liter of water. If sprayed at this rate, no weed infestation occurs for 45 days. The first weeding should be done after one to one and a half months and the second after one month. Fertilizer should be applied at the time of weeding and watered immediately.
Crop protection
Karpa: For control, Dithan M-45 should be sprayed at an interval of 10 to 15 days by mixing 30 grams per 10 liters of water.
Aphids: Dimethoate 30 e.c. for control. 15 ml or Lambda Cilothrene 5 cc. 6 ml or quinolphos 25 e.c. These insecticides should be sprayed alternately by mixing 25 ml per 10 liters of water.
Harvesting and production
Onions should be harvested after 50% of the onion heads fall naturally. Without cutting the onion leaves, spread the onion in the steamer in such a way that one onion root is covered by another onion leaf. Onion leaves should be dried in the field for 3 to 5 days. After that dry onion leaves should be twisted and cut by keeping a distance of one inch from the stem.Then the onion should be dried in a thin layer (1-2 feet) in the shade of a tree or canopy for a period of three weeks. After that the onions should be graded, the rotten and broken ones should be discarded and medium sized uniform onions should be kept in the storage room.
Kharif: 40-60 quintals per acre
Creeping: 80-100 quintals per acre
Summer: 100-120 quintals peracre
