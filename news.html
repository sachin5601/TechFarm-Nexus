Reconciliation Scheme - There are often disputes between farmers regarding land possession and wahi-wati, now to settle these disputes, the Maharashtra Government has introduced the Reconciliation Scheme. The scheme will provide concession in registration fee and stamp duty for exchange deed of agricultural land holders if the possession of agricultural land in the name of one farmer is with another and if the possession of agricultural land in the name of the other farmer is with the first farmer. Stamp duty will be Rs.1000 and registration fee will be Rs.100 for exchange of deed registration. The scheme will create positivity among the farmers and the cases in various courts will be settled and there will be no unnecessary interference of land surveyors. üëâApplication Procedure - Talathi or Mandal Officer Agriculture Department
