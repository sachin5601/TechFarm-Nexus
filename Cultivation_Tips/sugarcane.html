Cultivation Tips For Sugarcane
Details
Farming Methods
Land
Land 100-150 cm medium, fully drained, 6.5-8 samu policy land should be selected. Test the planting soil.
Pre-cultivation
he soil should be deeply plowed twice vertically and horizontally. Break the clods and loosen the soil by giving 1-2 layers of kulwa as per requirement. Before planting sugarcane, the green crop should be buried in the ground with jute/Dhaincha. 8, 10 and 12 tonnes of cow dung, 3 tonnes of composted manure, 2.5 tonnes of pressed compost and 2 tonnes of vermicompost per acre for early, pre-season and late-season sugarcane respectively if no green crop is planted, half and the remaining half during the second plowing before sugarcane planting. Mix into the soil before release.
Distance between two rows – light soil 90 cm, medium soil 100 cm, heavy soil 120 cm
caste
Aadsali – Ko 86032, KoM 0265, KVSI 9805
Preseason – KO 86032, KOM 0265, KVSI 9805, VSI 434, KO 8014, KVSI 03102, KO 94012, KOSI 671, MS 10001
Suru – KO 86032, KOM 0265, KVSI 9805, VSI 434, KO 8014, KVSI 03102, KO 94012, KOSI 671, KO 92005, MS 10001
Planting time
re-season – 15th December to 15th February,
Pre-season – 15th October to 30th November
Off-season – 15th July to 15th August
Cultivation
Sugarcane should be cultivated using seedling, one-eye or two-eye tipri. 9-11 months old, healthy, juicy and pure, fresh, improved varieties of chickpeas grown in chickpea plantations should be used.
One eye - distance between two eyes should be 30 cm. Planting should preferably be done dry. Keep the eye separate from the upper side and give water lightly or before planting the sugarcane, leave a little water in the rain and apply koli on the steam.
Two Eyes – Keep a distance of 15-20 cm between the two tips and make sure that both the eyes come to the side. For this, even wet infected banana will work. But care should be taken that the tipri is not pressed deeply. 10000 per acre for heavy land and 12000 for medium land.
Seedlings – Seedlings prepared using cocopeat in plastic trays are ready for planting in 30-45 days.
Number of sugarcane plants per acre according to spacing : 120 x 60 cm – 5500, 150 x 60 cm – 4450, 180 x 60 cm 3700, 240 cm x 60 cm – 2780, joint line 75 cm-150 cm x 60 cm – 5930, joint line 90 -180 cm x 60 cm – 4970
Seed processing
Before planting, dip 100 grams of carbendazim and 265 ml of dimethoate solution in 100 liters of water for 10 minutes and after chemical seeding, 10 kg of Acetobacter and 1.25 kg of phosphorus dissolving bacteria in 100 liters of water should be inoculated by dipping the sugarcane tips for 30 minutes.
Nutrient spray
After planting days after planting multimicronutrient and multimicro tree enter are sprayed with 500 500 water each and 7.5 days each 750 water at statutory rate.
Intercultivation
Weed management should be done by weeding as required. After 2 months after planting with the help of tools, weeding should be done by weeding. After sugarcane planting, spray 2 kg of Atrataf or 500 g of metribuzin mixed with 400 liters of water per acre when the land is still wet. If infestation of weeds like lavala or grass is found after sugarcane emergence, spray glyphosate 1 kg per acre mixed with 400 liters of water only on the weeds. For this, WFN40 nozzle should be used and plastic hood should be used on the nozzle.
Water management
Generally water beds should be 8 cm deep till large constructions. After that 10 cm depth should be given. Depending on the season, watering should be given after 8-10 days in summer, 14-15 days in monsoon and 18-20 days in winter. Using more water than this leads to salinization of the soil and also drains nutrients through runoff. Drip irrigation system should be used to save water and increase sugarcane production
cutting
Sugarcane should be cut according to season and maturity. Crop water should be stopped for 15 days before cutting. The cut sugarcane should be immediately sent for cutting. Sugarcane should be cut in the first 12-13 months, in the pre-season 14-15 months and in the off-season 16-18 months.
